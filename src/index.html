About Us

Hi There!
The IT Council is a group of people who have a passion for technology and dedication towards building a tech-loving community at DPS Bangalore South.
The IT Council aims to develop an interest in technology and to help create a gradual learning curve for our fellow students.
We host several tech competitions and seminars within the school to increase our fellow students' critical thinking, problem-solving skills and digital skills. This is of great importance in the contemporary world as in everything has now moved online.
Along with the resources provided and events conducted by the council, we aim to build a conducive environment for fellow students to mutually learn, teach, grow and explore the world of tech!
